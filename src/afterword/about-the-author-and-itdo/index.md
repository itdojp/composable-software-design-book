# About the Author and ITDO

This book is published under the ITDO Inc. name.
In this repository, ITDO serves as the authoring and editorial voice for a practical method that connects compositional reasoning to software architecture, AI-assisted delivery, and verification-aware engineering.

The manuscript is intentionally organization-facing rather than personality-driven.
Its claims are framed so they can be reviewed, adapted, and reused by engineering teams who need durable artifact boundaries more than they need a single author's personal workflow.
That is also why the book keeps the running example small, explicit, and repository-native.

Readers who use the book in team settings should treat the repository as the operational companion to the published prose.
The canonical English manuscript, example artifacts, QA scripts, and publication notes are all versioned together so that conceptual changes and artifact changes remain inspectable in one place.
